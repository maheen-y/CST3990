Welcome to our Computing Skills Quiz

This tech skills quiz has been designed for female students to help them explore skills required in technology related degrees at Middlesex University. This activity aims to engage females into technology degrees and help break barriers, encouraging a gender diverse and inclusive workforce. You can also view technology related degrees offered at Middlesex University by clicking on the study at MDX tab or explore job opportunities by selecting the employability tab.

Below are some examples of famous female pioneers in Technology who are brilliant role models for females in the present day

  1. Ada Lovelace - Known as the first Computer Programmer, her work laid the foundations for modern Computing
  2. Grace Hopper - A revolutionary Computer Scientist, she was instrumental in developing the Common Business Oriented Language (COBOL) which is still used today
  3. Radia Perlman - A Network Engineer who designed the algorithm for Spanning Tree Protocol, she was nicknamed 'Mother of the Internet'
  4. Dina St Johnston - Founded the UK's first software house in the 1950s named Vaughan Programming Services. One client was British Rail, the company developed software to display real-time information for passengers
